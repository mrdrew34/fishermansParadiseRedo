The Fisherman's Paradise

We have been serving the local area for more than 34 years, and have come to be known as the go to fishing supply store. Our speciality is freshwater baits and lures. We also carry an assortment of poles and reels to help assist in catching that almost always elusive "monster".

Manufacturers:

We only carry products from the best fishing supply manufactures. Some of these manufactures include: Browning, Abu Garcia, Offshore Angler, Accurate, Penn, Rapala, Arbogast, Bandit and Bill Lewis.

Lures:

Our lure selection is second to none. We carry every type of lure that would be needed; soft baits, hard baits, spinnerbaits and buzzbaits, bass jigs and flies.

Live Bait:

If live bait is what you are looking for, we have you covered there too. We carry night crawlers, jumbo panfish worms, leeches, butter worms, wax worms, spikes, meal worms, giant meal worms, extra bright maggots, crickets and spawn.

As you can see we have an outstanding selection, but if we do not have what you are looking for we will gladly assist in getting whatever you need!
